Why CGT Matters

Crypto is taxed in Australia under capital gains rules. A CGT event may happen when you:

  • Sell or gift crypto
  • Swap tokens
  • Convert to AUD or buy something

CGTools helps you interpret your transaction history, not lodge your tax return.

Who Built This?

Not Financial Advice

CGTools is an educational tool. It doesn't auto-lodge anything or provide official tax advice. Use it to get clarity, not as a substitute for a tax agent.
