Why CGT Matters

Crypto is taxed in Australia under capital gains rules. A CGT event may happen when you:

  • Sell or gift crypto
  • Swap tokens
  • Convert to AUD or buy something

CGTools helps you interpret your transaction history, not lodge your tax return.
